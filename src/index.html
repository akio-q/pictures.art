Frequently Asked Questions

Answer any of your questions

How long does it take to produce an order?

The turnaround time depends on three factors: the complexity of the painting, the style of painting and the size. The average turnaround time is 7 days, excluding delivery time In some cases, at the request of the customer can reduce the period to 5 days.

Note: If you decide to change something in the painting, make adjustments - it may take additional time, which is not taken into account in the agreed time of fulfillment.

Will I be able to see the painting before I receive it?

Yes. When ready, we send a digital photo of your painting by e-mail so that you can make adjustments and requests, if any. You will also be able to see the painting "live" in our salon. Please let your manager know.

What format the photos should be

We work with all known image formats! Through the site you can upload JPEG, TIFF, PNG up to 50 megabytes in size. You must own the copyright or have permission to use the image. If you have a different image format (RAW, PDF), you can use the FTP uploader. If you want to make a photo from a paper photo (not digital), send it to us, we will scan, process and return the photo with your order!

May I give special instructions to the artist

When you place your order on the site, you can add your comments in a special text field. You can also: combine objects from different photos into one or delete objects from a photo, you can change the background, change clothes, outfits, make a color picture from a black and white photo, as well as order a picture from a damaged or old photo.
